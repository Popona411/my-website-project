About Pona Vocational Ltd

Pona Vocational Ltd is a modern vocational institute dedicated to training the next generation of professional welders and metal fabricators. Our training combines practical hands-on workshops, industrial exposure, and technical theory.

Our mission is to equip students with skills that are highly demanded in construction, oil & gas, manufacturing, and engineering industries.

Our Training Programs

Arc Welding

Learn modern arc welding used in construction and metal industries.

Metal Fabrication

Design and build metal gates, structures and equipment.

Gas Welding

Professional oxy-acetylene welding techniques.

Industrial Safety

Learn workplace safety standards used in industries.
